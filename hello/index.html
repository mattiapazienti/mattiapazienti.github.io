The re-descovery of music

2006 Latina, Italy

Avid listener of post-hardocore, punk and metalcore I decided to have a say in music, after a failed attempt as drummer I switched focus to synthesizers and joined my friends in their post punk band, doing some touring and stuff.

Sweden part one

2005 Karlstad

After one of many summers in Sweden I decided to stay and moved to Karlstad for six months, ended up doing some web design and hanging around the university, moved then back to Italy in December.

The discovery of music

1998 Aprilia, Italy

After a childhood spent with lego, videogames and soccer It was finally the time to discover all the great things behind music, thanks to my sister I started as a late fan of Nirvana and Smashing Pumpkins.

My first PC

1995 Aprilia, Italy

For as little as 1000$ (2 million italian lire) a brand new compaq presario is placed in the hallway in our home, I remember neverending nights playing King's Quest, Loderunner and PFA Player of the year. This was also the time I started creating websites with excel...

Volkswagen Caravelle!

1987 Aprilia, Italy

After years of service my family dismiss the beloved Ford Taurus and goes on to buy a "hippie" minivan, a blue volkswagen caravelle, our long distance travels to Sweden reach a whole new level!

Hello world!

1982 Velletri, Italy

Here I am, born on November 10th, two months ahead of scheduled date, guess I couldn't resist to get out, or maybe I might just like even numbers...

Semola

2012 Aprilia, Italy

Found in the parking lot of the local mall, Semola is our cat. He was just waiting for us out there.

Sweden part two

2014 Stockholm, Sweden

Maybe it's time again, this time Stockholm, following the steps of my parents, who settled here for seven years in the seventies, it felt about right to make the move, something new and still something so familiar, things can happen here!

USA

2016 USA

Trip of a lifetime, after one year of planning me and my wife are ready to sail off to the states, a seventeen days long road trip from florida to california

Back to school

1986 Aprilia, Italy

One year later than all the other kids, italian was not my strongest suit at age 4; but swedish was, none of the other kids understood a word of mine.

High school

1997 Aprilia, Italy

So this is it, high school, five neverending years. I was lucky to have a friend, the only one in there: drawing sketches on books and doing music talk made it all easier.

Pantheon Multimedia

Freelance

Senior Frontend Developer

Coding HTML and CSS plus Javascript at Banzai Media for large part of the company portfolio, including Giallozafferano.it, Studenti.it, Liquida.it and more.

Frontend Lead

2014 Stockholm, Sweden

Code, structure, documentation, a/b testing, wireframing and mockups at Metro Nordic AB, with focus on mobile usability and optimization for diverse products as metro.se, allastudier.se, metrojobb.se and studentkortet.se.

1995

The art of making

The year we got the first Personal Computer, it didn't took me much to understand that there was more in it than just games. Creating designs and fake web sites with excel was my way to dream about crafting something useful to many.

2002

A deeper look into design

I finally had the chance to explore the diversity of design, three years at the Pantheon Multimedia Academy of Arts in Rome made it clear to me, you can delight people, you can ease their lifes or just make them feel better.

2006

The digital swing

I took a direction, the digital landscape was too promising and appealing with it's endless opportunities with music, media, data and services, working as a frontend developer, aiming to pixel perfection and a flawless experience, making it easy for users to interact with interfaces in any device.

2014

Things gets techy

Things are happening in Sweden, startups are on the rise with innovative yet familiar products everyone want's to use, everyday. I want to be there. Everything is more agile, it's not just about coding, it's about structure, planning and thinking, it's not about the product itself, it's about the user, the consumer.

2017

A new landscape

Things are getting more interesting than ever, wearables and VR are already here and things are happening really fast, it's not about the product, nor about the user, now it's about the reality, and how we interact with it.
